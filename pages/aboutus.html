Mng'aro Styles.

"What about Mng'aro
Styles?"

Mng’aro is a sheng word and simply means “clothing”.
Mng’aro styles is inspired by the traditional African-wear/Kitenge/Ankara culture, patterns and colors.

The African fashion trend lately, has created a buzz in the Fashion Industry/Market and its easily accessible in many ranging varieties such as; Bags, Backpacks, Earrings, Shoes, Bangles, Headbands and much more…, made out of the Kitenge/Ankara fabric. Most of the accessories are from African culture and they’re made out of the Kitenge/Ankara fabric.
